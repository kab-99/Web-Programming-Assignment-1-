Personal Development

Reflections and summaries from Chapters 4–7 of "Twelve Pillars" by Jim Rohn and Chris Widener.

Chapter 4 – Achieve Your Goals

The main point of this chapter is to clearly define your goals. Charlie teaches Michael that goals give direction and motivate people to become better versions of themselves. As without direction people will drift around aimlessly all while making little to no progress. The chapter also shows the importance of breaking long-term goals into smaller, achievable steps so progress can be made consistently.

Application: As an animation student, this chapter reminded me that I need to have a clear plan for my future. Unlike many traditional careers locally, becoming an animator often requires building a portfolio, improving my skills, and networking before finding work. Most of which can be set as milestones to achieve in order to become a fullyfledged animator.

Chapter 5 – The Proper Use of Time

This chapter focuses on using time wisely by prioritizing what is important instead of only reacting to issues. Charlie explains that discipline today prevents regret in the future and that every day should be spent working towards something worthwhile or beneficial.

Application: As a college student, I often have assignments and deadlines that take up most of my time. However, this chapter made me realize that I should also make time to improve my animation skills outside of class. This will allow for a much smoother transition into the industry post-graduation.

Chapter 6 – Surround Yourself with the Best People

This chapter teaches that the people we spend the most time with have a strong influence on our attitudes, habits, and overall success. Charlie encourages Michael to surround himself with positive and like-minded people who inspire him on his own journey toward success.

Application: This lesson applies to me because the animation industry is very competitive and constantly changing. Being around classmates, instructors, and other artists who are passionate about improving their craft will encourage me to keep learning and developing my own skills.

Chapter 7 – Be a Life-Long Learner

This chapter explains that learning should never stop. Successful people continue reading, practicing, and improving throughout their lives because knowledge and skills must constantly grow.

Application: This chapter is especially relevant to me because animation is always evolving with new software, techniques, and technologies. Even after college, I will need to continue learning to stay competitive in the industry. I want to keep improving my drawing, animation, and storytelling skills so I can build a successful career and adapt to future changes in the field.
