Creativity Drawing is one of my favorite hobbies and plays a major role in my goal of becoming an animator[cite: 1, 2]. Because I enjoy it, practicing and completing art-related assignments feels less like work and more like something I genuinely want to do[cite: 2].

My Hero

My hero is my mother. She is hardworking, determined, and always puts her family first. No matter how tired she is, she continues working to ensure that I am cared for and supported.

"By the sweat of your brow, one shall eat bread."

She lives by these words every day through her dedication and perseverance. She inspires me to stay disciplined, work hard, and continue pursuing my goals even when challenges arise.

AI Business of 2025

My favorite AI business idea is AI-powered content creation. These tools help users create images, videos, designs, and written content more efficiently. As an aspiring animator, I believe this technology can help generate ideas, create references, and speed up repetitive tasks, allowing artists to spend more time focusing on creativity and storytelling[cite: 1].

One ethical concern is that AI systems may use artists' work without permission. Another concern is that some companies may rely too heavily on AI instead of human creators. To address these issues, companies should use training data ethically, give proper credit where necessary, and use AI as a tool to assist creators rather than replace them.

Example: Canva AI, which helps users create designs and visual content using artificial intelligence.
